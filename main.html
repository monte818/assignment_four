  • Plan Your Visit Ahead of Time: Sequoia National Park is a vast and diverse park, so plan your visit in advance. Check the park's website for the latest information on road conditions, weather, and any alerts or closures.
  • Explore the Giant Sequoias:Sequoia National Park is home to some of the largest trees on Earth, including the famous General Sherman Tree. Be sure to visit the Giant Forest, where you can marvel at these enormous trees.
  • Hiking Safety:If you plan to go hiking, research the trails beforehand and choose ones that match your fitness level and interests. Be prepared with proper hiking gear, including water, snacks, and clothing suitable for the season.
